In addition to search, ((es3)) offers several options for analyzing and visualizing your data.

- <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-aggregations" text="Aggregations"/>: Use the ((es)) REST API to summarize your data as metrics, statistics, or other analytics.
- <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-discover-your-data" text="Discover"/>: Use the **Discover** UI to filter your data or learn about its structure.
- <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-visualize-your-data" text="Visualizations"/>: Build dynamic dashboards that visualize your data as charts, gauges, graphs, maps, and more.
- <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-alerting" text="Alerts"/>: Create rules that trigger notifications based on your data.
