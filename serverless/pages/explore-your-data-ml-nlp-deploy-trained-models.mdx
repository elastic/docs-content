If you want to perform ((nlp)) tasks in your cluster, you must deploy an
appropriate trained model. There is tooling support in
[Eland](https://github.com/elastic/eland) and ((kib)) to help you prepare and 
manage models.

1. <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-ml-nlp/deploy-trained-models/select-model">Select a trained model</DocLink>.
1. <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-ml-nlp/deploy-trained-models/import-model">Import the trained model and vocabulary</DocLink>.
1. <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-ml-nlp/deploy-trained-models/deploy-model">Deploy the model in your cluster</DocLink>.
1. <DocLink slug="/serverless/elasticsearch/explore-your-data-ml-nlp/deploy-trained-models/try-it-out">Try it out</DocLink>.
