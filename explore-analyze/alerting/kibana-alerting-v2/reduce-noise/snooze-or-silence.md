# Snooze or silence {{kib}} alerting v2 alerts [snooze-or-silence-v2]

**Snooze**, **silence**, and **acknowledge** are ways to temporarily suppress notifications for {{kib}} alerting v2 **alert episodes** or **series**. Each operates at a different scope.
