Width
:   Control how much horizontal space the control occupies:

    - **Minimum width**: set a minimum width. The final width can still vary depending on the other controls and their width settings.
    - **Expand width to fit available space**: fill the remaining horizontal space on the dashboard.

    Where you set it:

    - {applies_to}`serverless: ga` {applies_to}`stack: ga 9.4` In the control's {icon}`gear` **Display settings**.
    - {applies_to}`stack: ga 9.0-9.3` In the control editor.
