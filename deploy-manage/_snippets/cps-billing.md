During technical preview, there are no separate {{cps}} charges.

When {{cps-init}} becomes generally available, usage is metered and charged based on the volume of data retained in your linked projects each month. Charges are billed to the origin project. The rate varies by the type of each linked project, such as {{obs-serverless}}, {{sec-serverless}}, or {{es-serverless}}. For {{obs-serverless}} linked projects, the rate also depends on the type of retained data.

{{cps-cap}} queries also incur a separate data egress charge for data transferred as a result of those queries. This appears as a separate line item on your bill.

Starting on August 1, 2026, before billing starts, your [usage page](https://cloud.elastic.co/usage) shows a billing preview with projected {{cps}} charges based on your current usage. No charges apply until billing begins.

To estimate monthly spend based on your linked project configuration, use the [{{ecloud}} pricing calculator](https://cloud.elastic.co/pricing).
